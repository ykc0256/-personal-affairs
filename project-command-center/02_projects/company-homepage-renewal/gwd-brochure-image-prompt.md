- One A4 vertical brochure page.
- Korean corporate engineering brochure.
- Professional environmental engineering company style.
- Technical, credible, and executive-friendly.
- It should look suitable for a government project presentation, executive review, and technical partner meeting.

- Dark navy header.
- White or very light gray body.
- Cyan, teal, blue, and restrained green accents.
- Realistic water treatment plant or 3D plant layout visual.
- Technical engineering grid, connected workflow lines, thin-line icons, and metric cards.
- Clean and structured grid layout.
- No cartoon.
- No generic AI robot.
- No decorative blobs.
- No purple-heavy gradient.
- No playful illustration style.

- Large Korean title: "GWD 기반 하·폐수 처리장 설계 자동화"
- Subtitle under the title.
- Right side visual: realistic 3D water treatment facility or technical plant layout.

Explanatory text density:
- This should feel like a real corporate technical brochure, not just a workflow poster.
- Include 2-3 very short Korean text blocks with actual explanatory copy.
- Each text block should be only 1-2 short lines.
- Keep the tone technical, credible, and grounded in current capability.

"GWD는 수처리 설계 업무의 반복 수정과 산출물 불일치를 줄이고, 설계 검토 속도와 일관성을 높이는 기반 엔진입니다."

- Clean Korean sans-serif typography.
- Large readable Korean text.
- Strong hierarchy.
- Use short paragraph blocks, concise labels, and diagram text.
- Do not use tiny placeholder text. Korean copy should be readable.

- Make all Korean text professional and legible.

Overall mood:
Reliable, technical, infrastructure-focused, engineering-grade, and credible.
